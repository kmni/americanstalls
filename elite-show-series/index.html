Elite Show Series

Available colors:

Frame

  • 14 Gauge Galvanized and Powder Coated Steel with an Open Rounded Yoke
  • Yoke is one piece of steel tube, no additional corner welds or sharp edges
  • Bars are evenly spaced 2 inches apart and fully welded inside frame
  • Each panel has 4 places for hanging feed/water buckets etc.
  • 2, 3 and 4 prong connector pins connect frames to each other
  • 10 feet length x 7 feet height x 1 inch width
  • Fronts = 130lbs
  • Sides (bars on top) = 110lbs
  • Backs (full Vinyl) = 85lbs

Vinyl

  • 1400gsm Vinyl coated Nylon webbing
  • 2 pieces of Vinyl ? Outside and Inside the stall
  • 2 inch thick Nylon sewn onto the edges and screwed into the steel frame
  • Round head screws and washers every 8 inches
  • 5 layers of industrial strength foam between the Vinyl

Door

  • Centered swinging doors that are 4 feet wide
  • Door hinges are fully welded and require no additional assembly
  • Door latch is fully welded to the frame
  • Door can be locked with a standard double end snap
  • Additional pin lock on the bottom of the door
